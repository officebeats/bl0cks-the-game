# Deception Arc — Factions (Override)

## The Commission
The old guard. They run the South Side through proxies and shell companies.
In the Deception Arc, they've placed operatives in EVERY faction — including yours.
Their people are trained to show high loyalty while working against you.
- **Specialty:** Infiltration, long-term manipulation
- **Hidden trait:** Commission agents have hidden loyalty 4+ below visible

## CPD (Chicago Police Department)
The cops are compromised. Some are on the take, some are undercover.
In the Deception Arc, CPD is a wildcard — you can't tell who's corrupt and who's clean.
- **Specialty:** Surveillance, heat generation
- **Hidden trait:** CPD-aligned characters generate extra Heat when they betray

## Federal Task Force
New to the Deception Arc. The Feds have been running a 2-year investigation.
They have 3 agents embedded across all factions.
- **Specialty:** Long-term extraction, evidence gathering
- **Hidden trait:** Federal agents have a special `secretAllegiance: 'federal'` that overrides all other loyalties
- **Trigger:** If Heat reaches 18+, they break cover and trigger a raid

## The Unknown
A shadow faction that only appears in D2 (The Double).
They don't control territory — they control people.
- **Specialty:** Creating double agents, mirrored identities
- **Hidden trait:** Their agents exist as TWO cards simultaneously
