# Level D3 — The Reckoning (Boss)

## Parameters
- Clock: 14
- StartingInfluence: 3
- StartingHeat: 6
- Territory: Englewood, Auburn Gresham, Chatham, Woodlawn
- Factions: The Commission, The Lords, The Stones, CPD, Federal
- Difficulty: Hard (Boss)

## Narrative Hook
The Commission knows about your operation. The Feds are circling. Your own people are selling information to three different factions simultaneously.

This is the final level of the Deception Arc. Every hidden stat in the game is now active. Every NPC has a secret allegiance. The AI is playing against you with full information while you operate in the dark.

You have one advantage: The Ledger. Everything you learned in D1 and D2 carries forward. Every grudge, every debt, every burned bridge — the AI remembers it all, and so should you.

The only way to win is to stop trusting loyalty scores entirely and play the information game.

## Starting Hand
- People: Alderman Ray "Kingpin" Okafor (Broker, Loyalty 10, Hidden: 2)
- People: Detective Maria Vasquez (Informant, Loyalty 4, Hidden: 8)
- People: "Little Mike" Thompson (Enforcer, Loyalty 6, Hidden: 6)
- People: Carmen "Numbers" Reyes (Runner, Loyalty 5, Hidden: 9)
- Move: SNITCH

## Special Rules
- Boss level: ALL Gambit options are available on every People Card decision
- Every NPC has a hidden loyalty that differs from visible by 3-8 points
- Three NPCs have secret Federal allegiance (they report Heat directly)
- Intel costs 0 Influence (free) for the first 3 uses, then costs 2
- The Ledger from D1 and D2 is injected into the AI prompt — past decisions matter
- WAR generates +3 Heat instead of +1 (Feds are watching)
- PEACE alliances can be broken by any faction at any time (trust is gone)
- If Heat reaches 18+, a Federal raid triggers — lose all contested territories

## Win Conditions
- Control 3 of 4 territories
- Identify at least 2 of 3 Federal agents (via Intel or SNITCH)
- Heat below 15 at game end

## Loss Conditions
- Heat reaches 20 (Federal intervention — game over)
- Control 0 territories at any point
- Clock expires with fewer than 3 territories controlled

## Boss Reward
- Completing The Reckoning unlocks the "Shadow Network" asset:
  Icon: 🕸️
  Effect: "All People Cards start with 1 hidden stat pre-revealed. Intel costs are halved."
  This asset persists into the main campaign.
