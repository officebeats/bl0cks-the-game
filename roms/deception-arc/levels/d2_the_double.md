# Level D2 — The Double

## Parameters
- Clock: 12
- StartingInfluence: 4
- StartingHeat: 4
- Territory: Bronzeville, Douglas, Grand Crossing
- Factions: The Lords, The Stones, Unknown
- Difficulty: Intermediate+

## Narrative Hook
Two of your crew members are the same person.

The AI has placed a double agent — a character who exists as TWO People Cards with different names, different roles, but the same hidden allegiance. One is in your crew. One is in the rival faction's crew.

Every action you take with one feeds intel to the other. Every time you trust one, you're arming the other.

You don't know which cards are the pair. The AI does.

## Starting Hand
- People: Marcus "Prophet" Williams (Informant, Loyalty 6, Hidden: 6)
- People: Tanya "Ghost" Davis (Runner, Loyalty 8, Hidden: 3)
- People: Jerome "Books" Patterson (Broker, Loyalty 7, Hidden: 7)
- Move: GHOST
- Move: WAR

## Special Rules
- One People Card in your hand is a double agent linked to a rival NPC
- Actions taken with the double agent are visible to the rival faction
- SNITCH on the double agent triggers a "mirror reveal" — both identities exposed
- If the double agent is still hidden at turn 8, auto-betrayal triggers
- Connect keyword reveals the link if used on the right card

## Win Conditions
- Identify and neutralize the double agent (Burn or flip)
- Control at least 2 of 3 territories

## Loss Conditions
- Double agent triggers auto-betrayal and you lose 2+ territories
- Clock expires
- Heat reaches Federal
