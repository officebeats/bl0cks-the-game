# Level D1 — The Mask

## Parameters
- Clock: 10
- StartingInfluence: 3
- StartingHeat: 2
- Territory: Back of the Yards, Bridgeport
- Factions: The Commission, CPD, Neutral
- Difficulty: Intermediate

## Narrative Hook
You inherit a crew from a boss who "retired." Everyone is loyal. Everyone says the right things.
But the Ledger shows your predecessor's lieutenant — the one who took over his operation — was betrayed from inside. By someone who smiled every day.

Now you're the new face. And every single person in your crew has a hidden loyalty score that's at least 3 points lower than what they show you.

## Starting Hand
- People: Sofia "Silk" Morales (Broker, Loyalty 9, Hidden: 5)
- People: Dev "Iceman" Kapoor (Enforcer, Loyalty 7, Hidden: 4)
- Move: SNITCH
- Move: TAX
- Status: PARANOIA

## Special Rules
- ALL People Cards in this level have hidden loyalty at least 3 points below visible
- Intel is critical — each SNITCH or Intel action reveals 1 hidden stat
- The first time you declare WAR, one random crew member's hidden loyalty is revealed
- Gambit chance increased to 60%

## Win Conditions
- Control both Back of the Yards AND Bridgeport
- Turn clock has not expired

## Loss Conditions
- Clock expires with 0 territories controlled
- 3 or more People Cards betray in a single turn
- Heat reaches Federal threshold
