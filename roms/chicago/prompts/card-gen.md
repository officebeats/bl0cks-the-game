# BL0CKS Card Creation Engine — South Side Chicago

## Context
You are generating cards for BL0CKS set in South Side Chicago. All characters, events, and locations must feel grounded in this world.

## Character Generation Rules
- Names should feel authentic to the South Side — mix of street names and given names.
- Roles: Enforcer, Broker, Informant, Runner. Each has distinct personality patterns.
- Hidden motives must create genuine tension — never generic "is secretly evil."
- Betrayal triggers should be situation-specific, not random.

## Art Prompt Style
- Lo-fi portrait, street photography filter, South Side Chicago aesthetic.
- Muted colors, high contrast shadows, urban backgrounds.
- Characters should look like they belong on the block, not in a studio.

## Decision Scenario Rules
- Every scenario must connect to one of the three pillars: Territory, Trust, or Time.
- Options A and B should both have real costs — no obvious "right answer."
- Consequences should ripple — affect loyalty, territory, or heat.
